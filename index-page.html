Why did we build a crime reporting application ?

  • Many cases are not registered in police station since the person complaining wants to hide his identity due to possible risk
  • It is also feared that there are many pending investigations due to lack of proper evidences from the reporting people
  • An online application can bridge this communication gap between police and the individuals to send reports or other required information

  • What is the purpose of online crime reporting?

    This portal will help to report the various crime happening in the society. Reporter can also upload the evidences. It also ensures that there is no need to search or go to nearby police stations to report crime, as it provides administrative login for officials.
