OUR MISSION

Stop Crime, before Crime stops you.

The advent of technology has opened new fronts of opportunities of leveraging on the diverse benefits of information technology to crime reporting. There are various technology platforms that have been developed to assist how crime incidences are reported, responded to and investigated. The technological applications have gone from telegraph, special radiocommunication, and dedicated phone lines to a more responsive and more pervasive technological application platforms (web and mobile software applications).

Despite the variety of proposed crime reporting systems in the, there is need for a robust crime reporting system that will provide easy access and usage alongwith highly secured database to store criminal records such that crime reporting can be done in a very short span and if required an immediate response from the police can be actualized using the same system. It is on this premise that the innovative approach Crime Reporting System (CRS) has been developed.
